Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant based in Chicago,Illinois,owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. Mario has an experience as a chef in Italy.The chefs draw inspiration from Italian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu that they rotate seasonally.The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.
